Custom cabinet estimate workflow

Measure from a customer photo without pretending pixels are magic.

A single normal photo cannot produce trustworthy real-world measurements by itself. This app solves that the honest way: the customer provides one known measurement in the same plane, then the tool calculates scale and estimates the cabinet door.

Upload a photo to begin. Best results: hold the phone square to the door/opening and include a tape measure, ruler, or known cabinet opening in the same flat plane.

Reality check: photo estimates should be treated as starter quotes. Final production still needs confirmed measurements because angle, lens distortion, and out-of-plane references can lie like a cheap tape measure.

How the measurement logic works.

  • Customer uploads or takes a photo.
  • Customer enters a known measurement, like a 12-inch ruler or existing cabinet opening width.
  • Customer clicks two calibration points on that known reference.
  • Customer clicks width and height points for the desired door/opening.
  • The app calculates inches from calibrated pixels and produces a configurable estimate.
